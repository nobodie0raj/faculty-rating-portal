How to Use the Faculty Review Portal

Welcome to the Faculty Review Portal! Follow these simple steps to provide your valuable feedback:

Steps to Give a Rating:

  • Navigate to the Review section from the main menu.
  • Select the subject you want to review.
  • Find the faculty member associated with that subject.
  • Rate them based on different criteria such as Teaching, Test Discussion, Doubt Solving, and Availability.
  • Each category has a rating scale from 1 (Poor) to 5 (Excellent).
  • Once you have provided ratings for all relevant subjects, click the Submit Review button.

Understanding the Rating System:

  • 1 Star ⭐ - Poor
  • 2 Stars ⭐⭐ - Fair
  • 3 Stars ⭐⭐⭐ - Good
  • 4 Stars ⭐⭐⭐⭐ - Very Good
  • 5 Stars ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ - Excellent

Why Your Feedback Matters:

Your reviews help improve the quality of education by providing constructive feedback to faculty members. Be honest and fair in your ratings!
